Ubralod Shemwvari Qatami

Ingredients

  • Erti mtliani qatami
  • 400 grami gatarebuli kakali
  • xarchos suneli
  • dafquli wiwaka
  • yviteli yvavili
  • marili gemovnebit

Momzadebis wesi

  1. Shevwvat qatami
  2. Tafaze davakaramelizot xaxvi
  3. Xaxvs davamatot 1 chiqa fqvili
  4. xaxvs da fqvils davamatot 1 sufris kovzi tomati
  5. 400 gram kakals shevuriot xarchos suneli yviteli yvavili da witeli wiwaka am yvelafers davasxat neltbili wyali
  6. Es kakali davasxat xaxvs da tomats
  7. Am yvelafers tafaze davamatot qatami
  8. Kargad movuriot da davelodot 10-15 wuti
  9. 15 wutis shemdeg tqveni kerdzi mzad aris!
